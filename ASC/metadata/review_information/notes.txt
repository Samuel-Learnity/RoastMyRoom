Review Notes for Apple Review Team
====================================

DEMO ACCOUNT
No sign-in required. The app works fully without authentication.
Sign in with Apple is optional and used only for cross-device points sync.

HOW TO TEST
1. Open the app — you'll see a 3-slide onboarding
2. Tap "Scan my room" on the last slide
3. Grant camera access (or use photo library)
4. Take a photo of any room or pick one from the gallery
5. Wait ~3 seconds for AI analysis
6. View your score, roast, and detailed breakdown

FREE TIER
- 2 free scans per day
- 4 bonus points granted on first launch
- Overall score, style badge, and roast are always visible
- Sub-scores and personality section are blurred (premium feature)

IN-APP PURCHASES
- 3 subscription plans: Weekly (€4.99), Annual (€29.99), Lifetime (€49.99)
- 4 consumable point packs: 10/35/75/200 points
- Points can be used for extra scans beyond the daily limit
- Points can unlock full details of saved scans

SIGN IN WITH APPLE
- Completely optional
- Located in Profile tab
- Grants 4 bonus points on first sign-up
- Enables cross-device points synchronization via Supabase backend

NETWORK REQUIREMENT
The app requires an internet connection for AI room analysis (API call to backend).
Viewing saved scan history works offline.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
- OpenAI GPT-4.1 mini (via Supabase Edge Function, for room analysis)
- Supabase (authentication and points sync)
- Firebase Analytics (anonymous usage analytics only)

CONTENT
The AI generates humorous "roasts" about rooms. These are lighthearted design
critiques, never offensive toward people. The humor is about furniture and decor.
