## Tips for Screenshots

1. **Use a real device** (iPhone 16 Pro Max) for best quality
2. **Show premium content** — unlock everything for screenshots
3. **Use attractive rooms** with good scores (7-9 range, not 10 — shows credibility)
4. **Dark mode** — the app uses dark backgrounds, screenshots will stand out
5. **Add device frames** using tools like Rotato, Screenshots Pro, or AppMockUp
6. **Localize captions** — add text overlays in each language above the screenshot
7. **Keep status bar clean** — full signal, WiFi, charged battery, reasonable time
